welcome

Rig Africa - a place where prophets a built

Rig Africa is a sub-organization under the Rig Nation umbrella, a faith-based organization that seeks to spread the message of Christianity globally. Rig Africa focuses specifically on evangelism and support for Christians in Africa. Through training and development programs, mission trips, and outreach events, Rig Africa aims to equip Christians in Africa to become effective ministers of the gospel. The organization is committed to serving the people of Africa and making a positive impact in their communities, in line with the broader mission of Rig Nation.

Training People to be prophets and prophets to be people

RIG Nation was founded in 2007 by Tomi Arayomi with the vision to restore the apostolic and prophetic ministry back into the Body of Christ and to equip this generation to be in a position and condition for use by God. RIG stands for Restoring Issachar’s Generation, a mission formed by the scriptural Chronicles 12:32: “Of the sons of Issachar who had an understanding of the times to know what Israel ought to do…” RIG Nation is headquartered in London, United Kingdom and has a growing presence in Africa (Nigeria), Asia (Philippines), North America (USA) and Europe (United Kingdom).
